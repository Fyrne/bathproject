Where to Eat

Eat like the locals with our selection of Baths best independent restaurants below:

The Circus Restaurant

Independent restaurant serves modern European food, from a seasonally changing menu. A must try when in Bath!

Acorn Restaurant

An amazing vegitarian place, awarded a michlin star in 2018. Very reasonable prices for the quality. Be sure to book in advance.

Clayton Kitchen

Another of Baths hidden treasures.

Why not book your table now?

Magu Burger

A student faviourite, in the heart of Oldfield Park. One of the best burgers in Bath, hands down.
